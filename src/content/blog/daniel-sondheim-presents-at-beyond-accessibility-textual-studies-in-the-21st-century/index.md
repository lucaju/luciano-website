Daniel Sondheim presented a paper at INKE Textual Studies team’s conference [Beyond Accessibility: Textual Studies in the 21st Century](http://hcmc.uvic.ca/~etcl/wordpress/inke/projects/conferences/ "Text Studies Conference 2012, Victoria"), in Victoria, BC. The paper was written with the help of Geoffrey Rockwell, Stan Ruecker, Mihaela Ilovan, Luciano Frizzera, and Jennifer Windsor, and was entitled “From Print to the Web and Back: The Current State of Scholarly Editions.”

See more information at INKE website: [http://inke.ca/2012/06/09/daniel-sondheim-presents-at-beyond-accessibility-textual-studies-in-the-21st-century/](http://inke.ca/2012/06/09/daniel-sondheim-presents-at-beyond-accessibility-textual-studies-in-the-21st-century/)
